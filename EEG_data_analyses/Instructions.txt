The folder contains the mains scripts developed to analyses the eeg data and organized in steps:
- Preprocessing from raw data to clean epoched data;
- Individual headmodel building (with and without T1 scans);
- Mutual Information computing at source level;
- Interpolaration to localise sources of interest (left Audio and left Visual sources);
- Signal reconstruction procedure at the sources of interest;
- Phase coupling analysis procedure between the two sources of interest;
